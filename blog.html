First runner up for the Best Sommelier in America Competition in 2009

  • Fernando Beteta

Being a sommelier is more of a lifestyle than a job, believes Master Sommelier Fernando Beteta, a blog created for wine enthusiasts where he shares his deep-rooted passion for wine. Beteta has studied Hotel Management and apprenticed in high-end hotels throughout Europe and in Thailand. He decided to update his profession when the sommelier position at NoMI at Chicago’s Park Hyatt opened up. He later prepared for Master Sommelier exam and passed six years later.
